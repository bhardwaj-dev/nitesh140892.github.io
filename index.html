Q1. Any fee for registering in the Hackathon?

No. You do not have to pay to register yourself or your team.

Q2. Any specific qualifications needed to take part in the Hackathon?

It is open for all (Students, Working Professionals, and Startups)

How do I submit my idea? ”Complete Process”

You need to send your PPT of Idea submission to starternityhacks@internity.in with proper naming convention mentioned in the guidelines.
1. You have to register as a Leader and declare your Team Size.
2. Ask your team members to register(Same Google Form) as members.
3. One of your team members has to send the pptx format of IDEA to us on starternityhacks@internity.in
4. You will receive confirmation of your Idea submission, once we receive it.

Q4. Do we need to have the entire idea fully working?

The MVP(Minimum Viable Product)/ POC (Proof of Concept) of your product in the finale needs to be working and its future aspects can be explained to judges in your pitch deck.
Kindly make sure, the business viability of your product for the final pitch.

Q5. Are there any restrictions on using any technology or number of pre-built libraries?

There is no restriction to use any language, technology stack, or libraries. You can use any of them to create a web/mobile application and solutions.

Q.6. Do I have to bring my own Devices, Laptops, connectors, LAN wires, HDMIs or any other kind of Hardware Devices for the finale of Society 5.0 Hackathon?

Yes, you have to bring any required item, devices and connectors which you will be using to build your solution. However, Wifi and basic power connectors will be provided.

Q7. Will travel reimbursement be provided?

No, we do not offer any reimbursement

Q.8 How can I create my team for StarternityHacks?

In the google form for registration:
1. You have to register as a Leader and declare your Team Size.
2. Ask your team members to register(Same Google Form) as members.
3. For any confusion, immediately contact the support numbers or mail at starternityhacks@internity.in

Q.9. Will I be provided with Fooding and Lodging for the event?

Food will be provided as follows -
1 Lunch - 2 pm, 15th Feb 2020
1 Dinner - 8 pm, 15th Feb 2020
1 Breakfast - 8 am, 16th Feb
Detailed information related to food and lodging will be provided to the Participants who will be shortlisted.

Q.10. What is the venue for StarternityHacks?

It will be finalized soon.

Q.11. At what time shall I reach the venue for StarternityHacks?

The event will start at 9.30 am. Please reach at least 1 hour before for the venue related formalities.

Q.12. Do I need to bring any ID to the finale for StarternityHacks?

Yes, please bring any Government Approved Identification Documents such as Adhaar, Pan, Voter ID, Passport, Driving Licence, etc.

Q.13. Can I invite my friend/Colleague/Family Member/Guest as Visitors for the event? Are visitors allowed to the Venue?

Yes, you can invite your friend/colleague/family member and guest as Visitor, there will be a pass given to them, at the time of their entry to premises of Hackathon Venue.
