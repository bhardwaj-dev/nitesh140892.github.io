Q1. Any fee for registering in the Hackathon?

No. You do not have to pay to register yourself or your team.

Q2. Any specific qualifications needed to take part in the Hackathon?

Yes, you should be Engineering Students / Working Professionals with 0-2+ year experience.

Q3. What is the screening round?

After registration within 24 hr, you will receive a Test link from (starternityhack@internity.in) which you need to submit within 3 days.

Are there any restrictions on using any technology or number of pre-built libraries?

There is no restriction to use any language, technology stack, or libraries. You can use any of them to create a web/mobile application and solutions.

Do I have to bring my own Devices, Laptops, connectors, LAN wires, HDMIs or any other kind of Hardware Devices for the finale of Society 5.0 Hackathon?

Yes, you have to bring any required item, devices and connectors which you will be using to build your solution. However, Wifi and basic power connectors will be provided.

Q6. Will travel reimbursement be provided?

No, we do not offer any reimbursement

Q.7. How can I create my team for Society 5.0?

It’s an individual participation-based hackathon.

Q.8. Will I be provided with Fooding and Lodging for the event?

Food will be provided as follows -
1 Lunch - 2 pm, 15th Feb 2020
1 Dinner - 8 pm, 15th Feb 2020
1 Breakfast - 8 am, 16th Feb
Detailed information related to food and lodging will be provided to the Participants who will be shortlisted.

Q.9. What is the venue for Society 5.0?

It will be finalized soon.

Q.10. At what time shall I reach the venue for Society 5.0?

The event will start at 9.30 am. Please reach at least 1 hour before for the venue related formalities.

Q.11. Do I need to bring any ID to the finale for StarternityHacks?

Yes, please bring any Government Approved Identification Documents such as Adhaar, Pan, Voter ID, Passport, Driving Licence, etc.

Q.12. Can I invite my friend/Colleague/Family Member/Guest as Visitors for the event? Are visitors allowed to the Venue?

Yes, you can invite your friend/colleague/family member and guest as Visitor, there will be a pass given to them, at the time of their entry to premises of Hackathon Venue.

Q.13. I have a question that is not addressed here!

Please reach out to - mention the respective person

Q.14. How do I get my prize money and what is the crieteria of winning the prize?

You will be given problem statement and will be evaluated after solving the problem in given number of hours.As this is hiring Hackathon, you will be given prizes as joining bonus when you will join the company.
